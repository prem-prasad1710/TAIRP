Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta or guanciale
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Pecorino Romano cheese
  • 50g Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil the spaghetti until al dente, then drain.
  2. In a separate pan, fry the pancetta or guanciale until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, grated Pecorino Romano, Parmesan, minced garlic, salt, and black pepper.
  4. Combine the cooked spaghetti, crispy pancetta, and egg mixture in a pan over low heat. Toss until well mixed.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with extra cheese and black pepper.
